About Me

Peter | Founder, Digital Book Growth

Professional Book Marketing Strategist for Independent and Emerging Authors

With a specialized focus on book marketing and sales optimization, I serve as a strategic partner for authors seeking measurable growth in their publishing journey. My expertise centers on transforming individual titles into revenue-generating digital assets through disciplined, professional methodologies.

I operate exclusively with authors who are serious about treating their books as professional products. This means moving beyond basic promotion into structured systems that drive discoverability, authority, and conversion at every stage of the reader journey.

My background as a Book Specialist Marketer has equipped me to address the unique challenges independent authors face:

  • Limited visibility in a crowded marketplace
  • Fragmented and inconsistent marketing efforts
  • Difficulty standing out among thousands of new releases every month
  • Lack of a structured system that converts interest into actual sales
  • Absence of professional brand identity that commands reader trust

I solve these challenges by building tailored strategies that emphasize clarity, professionalism, and measurable results. Every author I work with receives a customized approach built around their specific book, genre, audience, and growth objectives.

Operating Philosophy

All client projects are managed with complete documentation and full transparency. Communication occurs solely through email to maintain a clear, auditable record of every strategy discussion, implementation step, performance update, and adjustment. This is not a casual service — every interaction is treated as a serious professional business relationship built for long-term author success.

Services

Strategic Book Growth Services

Each service is fully customized around your book's genre, target audience, competitive landscape, and specific growth objectives.

01

Book Launch & Positioning Strategy

A successful book launch does not happen by accident. It is the result of careful planning, strategic timing, and coordinated execution across multiple channels simultaneously.

  • Deep audience profiling to identify your buyers
  • Competitive analysis of your genre landscape
  • Compelling messaging for buyer motivations
  • Sales page optimization for conversion
  • Pre-launch momentum building
  • Launch day platform coordination
  • Post-launch sales velocity strategy

The outcome is a book that enters the market with authority, clarity, and a clear pathway to consistent sales from day one.

02

Sales Funnel Optimization

The gap between a reader discovering your book and actually purchasing it is where most authors lose sales. A poorly structured pathway creates hesitation and abandoned purchases.

  • End-to-end funnel from impression to purchase
  • Strategic landing page architecture
  • Precision calls-to-action placement
  • Reader preview confidence mechanisms
  • Seamless cross-platform purchase paths
  • Abandoned interest recovery strategies
  • Ongoing funnel analysis and refinement

The result is a system that works continuously to convert interest into revenue without requiring constant manual intervention.

03

Amazon & Platform Optimization

Your book's presence on major retail platforms is one of the most powerful sales assets you have. Most authors leave significant revenue on the table by neglecting these elements.

  • Comprehensive high-intent keyword research
  • Metadata refinement for discoverability
  • Conversion-focused description copywriting
  • Strategic category and subcategory selection
  • Review acquisition system development
  • Ongoing monitoring and optimization

The goal is to make your book the obvious choice when the right reader is searching for exactly what you have written.

04

Targeted Visibility Campaigns

Visibility without targeting is wasted effort. Reaching millions of uninterested people produces no sales. Reaching thousands of high-intent readers produces consistent revenue.

  • Precision audience research and location
  • Niche-calibrated paid and organic strategies
  • Multi-channel sustained exposure architecture
  • Creative asset guidance for promotions
  • Real-time campaign monitoring and adjustment
  • Detailed performance reporting with attribution

Every campaign is built around your book's unique market position and executed with measurable outcomes as the primary objective.

05

Author Brand Elevation

A single book is a product. An author brand is a legacy. Authors who invest in professional identity create compounding advantages for every future release.

  • Consistent author identity development
  • Professional digital channel positioning
  • Strategic presence and content architecture
  • Cross-title promotion frameworks
  • Long-term visibility planning beyond campaigns
  • Industry positioning for literary authority

The result is an author brand that commands respect, attracts loyal readers, and makes every future release easier to market.

06

Featured Author Placement

Selected authors receive premium placement within my professional network and targeted reader channels. This curated program delivers direct sales impact and enhanced visibility.

  • Prominent dedicated reader channel showcasing
  • Professional conversion-optimized presentation
  • Strategic established network exposure
  • Sustained multi-window visibility
  • Direct purchase pathway integration
  • Selective quality-maintained placement

Placements are offered selectively to maintain effectiveness. Featured authors represent the professional standard I deliver.

The Digital Book Growth Engagement Process

Every project follows a structured, documented workflow designed to protect both parties, ensure clarity, and deliver consistent professional results.

01

Initial Email Inquiry

You send a detailed email to peterdigutalsuite03@gmail.com outlining your book, your current situation, your platforms, and your specific growth objectives. All information shared is treated with complete confidentiality. Simply send a professional email with your details and I will take it from there.

02

Comprehensive Strategy Assessment

I conduct a thorough review of your book, genre, competitive landscape, and stated objectives. I then prepare a comprehensive response detailing the recommended strategic approach, key tactics, timeline expectations, and measurable success indicators. This becomes the foundation of our engagement.

03

Confirmation & Professional Onboarding

Upon mutual agreement via email, we confirm scope, deliverables, and responsibilities. Everything is recorded in writing before any work begins, ensuring both parties share a clear understanding of the engagement and expected outcomes.

04

Strategy Execution

I implement the agreed strategy with disciplined attention to detail. You receive regular email updates covering progress milestones, tactical adjustments, and intermediate performance indicators throughout the active campaign period. You are never left wondering what is happening.

05

Performance Review & Documented Reporting

Upon completion, I deliver a comprehensive performance review via email including data-backed insights, results against objectives, lessons learned, and strategic recommendations for continued growth. This documented record becomes a valuable asset for your publishing journey.

Communication Policy

Professional Communication Standards

Clarity, accountability, and professionalism define every interaction within a Digital Book Growth engagement.

Email-Only Communication

To maintain the highest level of clarity, professionalism, and accountability in all project engagements, every aspect of our working relationship is conducted exclusively through email.

This includes:

  • Initial inquiries and project discussions
  • Strategy development and presentation
  • Approvals, confirmations, and agreements
  • Progress updates and milestone reporting
  • Performance reviews and optimization recommendations
  • All correspondence related to your book and campaign
Phone calls, instant messaging, video meetings, and social media direct messages are not available for project discussions. This structure has been deliberately designed to deliver the best possible professional outcomes for every author I work with.
Complete Documentation

Every agreement, strategy decision, revision, and deliverable exists in written form. Nothing is left to memory or verbal interpretation.

Full Accountability

Both parties have access to a complete, timestamped record of every interaction. This protects you and ensures I am held to every commitment.

Professional Clarity

Written communication eliminates ambiguity that often occurs in real-time conversations, ensuring strategies are clearly understood and executed.

Focused Strategic Thinking

Email allows for thoughtful, well-considered responses rather than rushed real-time reactions, resulting in higher quality strategic guidance.
